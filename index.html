About Nicholas

Nicholas Satyahadi or so called Nicho pursued his interest in mathematics by majoring in applied mathematics at Pelita Harapan University. Throughout his studies, Nicho developed keen interest in both Actuarial Science and Big Data Analysis, thinking one day he would be able to combine those interests. Since then, he always tries to find opportunities to learn beyond what's thought in university by attending online courses or self-teaching.

Nicho is currently working as a Valuation Actuarial Analyst in Sun Life Financial Indonesia. He used to join one of the largest e-commerce platform in Indonesia, Tokopedia, as a Data Analyst Intern in Promotion Tribe. Needless to say, he has managed to experience both of his interests. Nevertheless, he always strives to overcome new challenges in his professional career.

Besides work..

Nicho likes to share and listen to thoughts about important topics that are relevant in the world, preferably with a glass of black coffee. Overall, he is a friendly and outgoing person but please do be aware of his unpredictable corny jokes :)

DataViz

DataViz is a web-based app created using R Shiny Dashboard. DataViz serves to aid users to visualize multiple plots using the same dataset. Moreover, the app also provides an interactive feature to ease the process in understanding the dataset.

Time Series Extrapolation

A simulation of extrapolation methods for time series datasets. The methods proposed in this simulation are constructed for both univariate and multivariate time series. The purpose of these extrapolation methods is to generate larger dataset from a small sample and keeps the original properties.
